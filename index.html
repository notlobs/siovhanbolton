My Story

Where journalism
meets strategy

My career started in a newsroom, where stories had deadlines, audiences had opinions, and everything had to work. That foundation shapes everything I do: I think in narratives, audiences, and what makes someone stop scrolling.

From producing Emmy-winning morning news to leading global social strategy for PMI, one of the world's largest professional associations, I've spent 10+ years at the intersection of creativity and results.

I built PMI's first comprehensive social listening program, launched their TikTok from scratch, and developed data frameworks that turn audience signals into decisions leadership can act on.

I live where brand, culture, and community collide. That's where the best social media happens.
